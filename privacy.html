Privacy Policy

Last Revised: May 3, 2026 | Version 2.0

Core Commitment: High Stars Aimers operates on a "Privacy by Design" principle. We believe your academic data is private property. We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third-party data brokers.

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how High Stars Aimers ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and shares your personal information when you visit our educational cloud platform or use our Resource Vault and Quiz Master services.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Identification Information

When you register for an Elite Account, we collect information that identifies you, including:

  • Full Name and Email Address.
  • Academic level (e.g., Secondary, Pre-University).
  • Login credentials (encrypted passwords).

2.2 Usage Data & Performance Metrics

To provide a personalized experience, we automatically collect data regarding your interaction with our tools:

  • Quiz scores and time spent on specific revision papers.
  • Device information (IP address, browser type, and operating system).
  • Cookies that track your session to maintain "Elite Access" across pages.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data for several critical purposes:

  • Service Delivery: Managing your account and providing access to the Resource Vault.
  • Personalization: Using performance metrics to suggest specific subjects in the Quiz Master.
  • Communication: Sending academic newsletters, system updates, or security alerts.
  • Analytics: Aggregating anonymous data to improve our STEM modules and overall platform speed.

4. Data Retention & Security

We retain your personal information only for as long as your account is active. All sensitive data is stored using AES-256 encryption. While we take every precaution, no system is 100% secure; we encourage users to use unique, strong passwords.

5. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you have the right to:

  • Request a copy of the data we hold about you.
  • Request the deletion of your account and all associated performance data.
  • Opt-out of any non-essential communications.

6. Contact Us

If you have questions regarding this policy or wish to exercise your data rights, please contact our Data Privacy Officer at high.stars.aimers.exam.notes@gmail.com.
