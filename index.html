Knowledge Base (FAQ)

Everything you need to know about the High Stars Aimers ecosystem.

What is High Stars Aimers?
High Stars Aimers is an elite, independent educational cloud platform. We aggregate premium revision materials, past papers, and expert notes from top-tier institutions into a high-performance digital workspace.
Is the platform free to use?
We offer a "Freemium" model. Basic revision papers are accessible to all, but "Elite Access" (which includes the Quiz Master, smart summaries, and advanced STEM guides) requires a subscription.
How does the Quiz Master work?
The Quiz Master uses an active-recall algorithm. If you get a question wrong, the system will re-introduce that topic more frequently until it detects you have mastered the concept.
Can I access resources without internet?
Yes. Elite members can download resources directly to their local storage via the platform dashboard for offline study during commutes or areas with poor connectivity.
